How it works

When you include a BarkGround Check with your rental application, it provides a landlord with peace of mind that they can welcome your dog without hesitation.

Additionally, your BarkGround Check can provide you with personalized recommendations for ways to improve your chances of getting your dog approved for the apartment or home of your dreams, and you will also be eligible for discounts and special offers at dog-friendly businesses in the BarkGround network.
